🛒 Shop For Coffee | Coffee Philosophy
Cozzee is a purpose driven specialty coffee company that allows you to impact the world by giving a significant portion of the proceeds from your purchase to a cause that you choose.

You buy some of the best coffee around. You choose one of 7 causes you are most passionate about. We ship your freshly roasted coffee straight to your door. Then we give a significant portion of the proceeds to our nonprofit partner leading a project in that cause area.

It's as simple as that
