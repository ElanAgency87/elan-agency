What We Do

A full management discipline, from first deal to long career.

01Brand Partnerships

Endorsements, ambassadorships, and long-form commercial deals with brands that align with our clients' values, market position, and career arc.

02Commercial Negotiation

Contract structure, fee architecture, usage rights, and the legal scaffolding behind every deal. We read the fine print, hold the line, and leave nothing on the table.

03Media & Communications

Press strategy, interviews, appearances, and proactive narrative management. The right exposure, on the right platforms, at the right moment.

04Speaking & Appearances

Keynote bookings, panel placements, hosting engagements, and appearance schedules built around audience quality, not volume.

05Personal Brand Strategy

Positioning, voice, and visual identity. A clear, consistent answer to who you are and why it matters, across every platform and every decade.

06Career Management

The long view. Five-year arcs, ten-year horizons, transition planning, and the decisions most agencies are too busy chasing the next deal to make.
