The Blend
A premium 70/30 coconut-soy wax blend for extraordinary scent throw and a clean 80 hour burn.
02
The Scent
Luxury fragrance oils added at exactly 65°C and stirred for two minutes to fully bind with the wax.
03
The Pour
Each candle hand-poured individually and allowed to cool undisturbed for 24 hours.
04
The Cure
A minimum two-week cure locks in full scent complexity before any candle leaves our hands.
