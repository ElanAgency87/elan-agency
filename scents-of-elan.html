Handcrafted

Made with
obsessive care.

01
The Blend
A premium 70/30 coconut-soy wax blend, selected for extraordinary scent throw and a clean, slow burn of up to 80 hours.
02
The Scent
Luxury fragrance oils added at exactly 65°C and stirred for two full minutes to fully bind with the wax.
03
The Pour
Each candle is hand-poured individually into its vessel and allowed to cool undisturbed for 24 hours.
04
The Cure
A minimum two-week cure locks in full scent complexity. No candle leaves our hands before it is ready.
